The Mission.

WhirlyOS is more than just a Debian fork. It's an educational experiment designed to remove the "fear" of Linux. By using cinematic visuals and playful animations, we create a focus-friendly environment for ADHD users and students.

Our Future Builds will feature local AI integration, helping you manage tasks and automate your workflow without sending data to the cloud. We believe in a private, playful, and powerful desktop.
