The Whirly Story

WhirlyOS began in 2025 under the name PixOS. It was a dream to merge the technical freedom of Linux with the cinematic wonder of Pixar.

Originally built on OpenSUSE, the project faced "broken build" challenges that led to a major strategic pivot. The creator moved to Debian Stable, utilizing the power of Cubic to craft a bespoke OS that isn't just about looks, but about focus.

Today, it stands as an ADHD-aware platform, proving that technology can be magical without being distracting.

FAQs

What is the base OS?
The core project scripts support Debian Stable, but our exclusive Flosshilde release is built on AnduinOS. This provides a highly compatible, Windows-style desktop container out of the box.
Why ADHD-Aware?
It features built-in screen timers, simple layouts, and a curated selection of tools to prevent "digital clutter" and hyper-fixation on the wrong things.
Is it official Pixar?
No. WhirlyOS is a fan-made, open-source project. "Find Your Spark" is inspired by the themes of 'Soul' but is not an official tagline.
