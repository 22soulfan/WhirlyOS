Installation Protocol

1. Download BalenaEtcher or Rufus.

2. Flash the WhirlyOS ISO to a USB.

3. Restart and enter Boot Manager (F10/F12).

sudo dd if=whirlyos.iso of=/dev/sdx bs=4M status=progress
