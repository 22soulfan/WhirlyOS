Focus through Play.

WhirlyOS was born from a simple observation: modern desktops are often either too clinical or too distracting. For the ADHD mind, a "boring" interface leads to friction, and friction leads to unfinished tasks.

We believe that an OS should feel like a creative companion. By building on the "Rock Solid" foundation of Debian Stable, we ensure your work is safe while providing a UI that feels alive.

Inspired by the cinematic warmth of films like Soul, our goal is to help you "Find your spark." We prioritize fluid animations, soft colors, and a "Notebook" philosophy where your digital space feels like a physical canvas.

Whether you're running a modern x64 workstation or breathing new life into a legacy i386 machine, WhirlyOS is here to keep your momentum going.
