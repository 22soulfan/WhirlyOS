Focus through Play.

WhirlyOS was born from a simple idea: technology shouldn't feel like a chore. For those of us with ADHD, traditional desktops can be either too distracting or too clinical. We build on Debian Stable to give you a "Rock Solid" foundation, then we wrap it in an interface that feels like a Pixar movie.

Inspired by the film Soul (2020), our motto "Find your spark" represents the journey of discovering passion through creation. Whether you're using our 64-bit modern builds or breathing life into an old i386 laptop, WhirlyOS is designed to stay out of your way while keeping you inspired.
