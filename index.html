How to Install

Prerequisites

Linux: Any distro + BalenaEtcher or DD.
Windows: 10/11 + Rufus or BalenaEtcher.
macOS: 10.15+ + BalenaEtcher.

Safety First

Install at your own risk. Warranty may be voided. Creator is not responsible for damage to your computer. Always shrink drives carefully for dual-booting.

Writing via Terminal (Advanced)

sudo dd if=path/to/whirlyOS.iso of=/dev/sdx bs=4M status=progress conv=fsync
