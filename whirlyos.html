The WhirlyOS Story

WhirlyOS was forked from the original hirohamada2014 repository to a new home under 22soulfan. It represents a shift towards cinematic computing.

Our mission is to support neurodivergent students by providing a desktop environment that is "Quiet" but "Inspiring."
